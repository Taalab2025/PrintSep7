Premium Business Cards

Make a lasting impression with our premium business cards. Perfect for professionals and businesses looking to stand out with high-quality printing that reflects their brand's excellence.

Key Features:

  • Full color CMYK printing on both sides
  • Premium 350gsm card stock
  • Choice of matte or glossy finish
  • Standard size (90mm x 50mm) or custom sizes available
  • UV coating option for extra durability
  • Rounded corners option available
  • Free design assistance if needed

Perfect For:

Business professionals, entrepreneurs, sales teams, networking events, trade shows, and anyone who wants to make a professional first impression.

File Requirements

Need help with design? We offer free design assistance for orders over 500 pieces!
Accepted File Formats:
  • PDF (Preferred)
  • AI (Adobe Illustrator)
  • PSD (Adobe Photoshop)
  • PNG (High Resolution)
  • JPG/JPEG (High Resolution)
  • SVG (Vector)
File Specifications:
  • Resolution: Minimum 300 DPI
  • Color Mode: CMYK (not RGB)
  • Bleed: 3mm on all sides
  • Safe Zone: Keep text 5mm from edges
  • File Size: Maximum 50MB per file
  • Fonts: Convert to outlines/curves or embed fonts
Design Tips:
  • Use high-contrast colors for better readability
  • Avoid very thin lines (minimum 0.25pt)
  • Black text should be 100% K only (not CMYK mix)
  • Include both front and back designs in separate pages/files
