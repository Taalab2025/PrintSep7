About PrintHub Egypt

Revolutionizing the printing industry by connecting businesses with quality printing services across Egypt

Our Story

How we started and where we're heading

Founded in 2024

PrintHub Egypt was born from a simple observation: businesses in Egypt struggled to find reliable printing services, compare prices, and manage their printing projects efficiently.

We created a platform that brings together the best printing vendors across Egypt, making it easy for businesses to get competitive quotes, compare services, and track their orders - all in one place.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of businesses across Egypt, from startups to large corporations, helping them save time and money on their printing needs.

Our Mission & Values

What drives us every day

Our Mission

To democratize access to quality printing services by creating a transparent, efficient marketplace that benefits both businesses and printing vendors.

Our Values

Transparency, quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction are at the core of everything we do. We believe in fair business practices and supporting local vendors.
